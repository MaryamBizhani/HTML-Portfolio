Sherry's Favorite Books

My top 3 Books of all-time

Little Prince

A classic novel written and illustrated by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry. This book is a deeply philosophical story that contains poignant observations about life, love, and human nature.

A Hundred Years of Solitude

This book is one of the most influential works of 20th-century literature and a cornerstone of magical realism, blending the extraordinary with the everyday in a seamless narrative. This novel is one of the greatest works of Gabriel García Márquez.

Dune

This is a science fiction book written by Frank Herbert. Set in a distant future where interstellar travel and feudal empires dominate, Dune is a complex story of politics, ecology, religion, and power.
